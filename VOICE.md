# VOICE

## Overview
Defines how the brand communicates across all written content.

---

## Tone Attributes

- Clear
- Confident
- Human
- Direct

---

## Writing Style

- Short sentences > long paragraphs
- Active voice only
- Avoid jargon unless necessary

---

## Audience Adaptation

### B2B
- More structured
- Data-driven
- Professional tone

### B2C
- Conversational
- Relatable
- Benefit-focused

---

## Messaging Pillars

1. Simplicity
2. Clarity
3. Value
4. Speed

---

## Examples

### Weak
"We aim to leverage innovative solutions..."

### Strong
"We help you build faster with AI."

---

## Do / Don’t

### Do
- Be specific
- Use real examples

### Don’t
- Over-explain
- Use filler words
